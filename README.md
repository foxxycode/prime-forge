# Prime Forge

Prime Forge is a revolutionary dual-collateral algorithmic stablecoin protocol that dynamically switches between over-collateralized and algorithmic modes based on real-time market conditions. The protocol utilizes a predictive stability algorithm that analyzes cross-chain market data, social sentiment, and macroeconomic indicators to proactively adjust supply and maintain price stability. It features a three-token ecosystem consisting of PRIME stablecoin, FORGE governance token, and FLUX volatility token, along with a unique Prime Score mechanism that personalizes yield rates and collateral requirements based on user behavior. Users can participate in Dynamic Yield Vaults that automatically rebalance across lending protocols and arbitrage opportunities using AI-driven optimization. The protocol integrates with real-world payment systems and includes comprehensive safety mechanisms such as emergency circuit breakers and time-locked governance controls.
